WHO I AM...

My name is Alexandra Cordero (she/her) and I am passionate about LGBTQ+ rights and activism. I am a high school student who has seen the impact of society on the freedom, comfort, and safety of my fellow LGBTQ+ peers. Through this website I aim to provide numerous resources for educating oneself on the differnet aspects of the community, for reaching out for help or guidance, and even for sharing personal stories.
