LGBTQIA2S+ Awareness

Bringing Awareness, Acceptance is a Given

WHAT

What is the point of all this? Well, this website is here to be a safe space to learn about the LGBTQ+ community, its misconceptions, and share important stories. You will also be able to find resources and other organizations/websites, and share any of your own concerns, questions, or stories. (Anonymously as well if you'd like!)

WHO

Who is this website geared/made/written for? Anyone! This website is made for all people, for those who are a part of the community and want to find a collection of resources or hear other stories, or for those who know nothing about the community but are willing to take some time to become more educated on the topic, or even for those who really don't care, and you somehow stumbled on this website.

EMPOWERING FIGURES

WHY

There are so many stereotypes, misconceptions, and lack of understanding of the LGBTQIA2S+ community. It is important to spread the word about what the LGBTQ+ commmunity truly holds and preventing the constant hate, misinformation, and stigmas surrounding the community. Through this website, we aim to educate, to combat the hate and violence, to spread useful resources, and share empowering or even relatable stories.
